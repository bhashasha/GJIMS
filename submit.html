Author Guidelines (Summary)

  • Word limit: 5,000–8,000 words (excluding references).
  • Use a recognised citation style (e.g., OSCOLA, APA, MLA).
  • Include an abstract and 4–6 keywords.
  • Ensure the file is anonymised for peer review.
  • Plagiarism must be below the journal’s accepted threshold.
